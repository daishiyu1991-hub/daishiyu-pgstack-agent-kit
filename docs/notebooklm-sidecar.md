# NotebookLM Research Sidecar

NotebookLM is not treated as a primary unattended collector.

It sits after confirmation and triage:

```text
confirmed high-value topic
-> NotebookLM-ready source bundle
-> supervised NotebookLM research
-> Obsidian Research Brief
-> PGBrain long-running topic candidate
```

Use it when:

- one story has strategic implications beyond a same-day headline
- several first-party sources need comparison
- the user wants deeper synthesis
- the topic may become a long-running notebook

Do not use it to lower the factual bar of the daily brief.
