# PGStack Brain Resolver

Use one primary home for each durable object.

- `agents/`: agent roles, hosts, and delegation patterns
- `ai-knowledge/`: reusable AI knowledge
- `business/`: business and market knowledge
- `entities/`: systems, tools, companies, and actors
- `memory/`: memory transport and governance
- `pipelines/`: recurring workflow specs
- `runbooks/`: operational recovery and execution notes
- `skills/`: skills, templates, and operating rules
- `sources/`: source summaries and evidence pointers
- `inbox/`: temporary holding area when the primary home is unclear
- `archive/`: superseded material

When unsure, write to `inbox/` and revisit during maintenance.
